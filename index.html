Toyota Supra Evolutions

This iconic lineup of Toyota Supras spans generations,
showcasing the evolution of performance and design from the MK1 to the MK5.

MK1 Supra

(1979 to 1981)

MK1 Supra

(1979-1981)

The MK1 Supra introduced the world to the Supra brand with its sleek, sporty design and reliable performance.

MK2 Supra

(1982–1986)

The MK2 Supra followed with a more refined look, offering increased power and improved handling.

MK3 Supra

(1986-1992)

The MK3 Supra brought turbocharged performance and a more modern design, becoming a true performance legend.

MK4 Supra

(1993-2002)

The MK4 Supra is arguably the most iconic, with its 2JZ engine and legendary tuning potential, cementing its status in the automotive world.

MK5 Supra

(2019–present)

Finally, the MK5 Supra marks a return to its roots with a bold, aggressive design and a turbocharged engine that combines modern performance with classic Supra DNA.
